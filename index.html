About Us

What We Do

We believe that 3D printing is the future of technology. To offer people an understanding of how amazing this new technology is, we provide a quick and affordable way for anyone to print anything. Our service allows you to print anything you want to be printed. All you have to do is send in an .stl file to our contacts, and we will give you a price (based on how much time and plastic it takes), and we will hand wrap and send to your address as fast as we can!

Why 3D Printing?

If you have never worked with 3D printing or have had something 3D printed, you will underestimate the power and potenial of 3D printers. Just the concept of turning a spool of plastic into anything you can imagine is incredible. This technology is already being used in hospitals for emergency situations, and some are even printing car and airplane parts. To introduce curious customers to this technology, every first order that you make comes with a 10% discount, so your first print is even cheaper and easier to get.
